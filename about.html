ABOUT⠀ABHIYAAN

Abhiyaan is Bharati Vidyapeeth College of Engineering’s Annual fest. Which is held in the month of February-March. We are non-profit volunteers whose main aim is to provide opportunities to the young talents of various colleges and to provide a positive message to the society. The funfilled event will be of 9-10 days consisting of all type of technical, sports, cultural and inter college events. There are three Cultural Days. Sports events are going to be held in a span of 4-5 days followed by our cultural event which is held in a span of next 2-3 days. Most importantly the event not only includes students from our campus (which includes Architecture, Engineering, Nursing, dental and diploma colleges) but also the students from near by colleges. So you can expect a foot fall of atleast 2000 to 2500 students.
